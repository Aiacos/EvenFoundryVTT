<objective>
Migrate `HUD_TILE_GEOMETRY` and the HUD raster frame constants from the PoC/simulator-only **288×144 / 576×288** geometry to the INV-2-verified hardware geometry **200×100 / 400×200** (RINV-02). The G2 image-container hard cap is 200×100 per tile, max 4 → a 400×200 raster region; full-screen raster is impossible. The on-screen placement of the 400×200 region inside 576×288 is parameterized (default deferred to Phase 20) — only the tile-internal geometry (200×100) and the relative 2×2 offsets are set here.

Purpose: Establish the correct hardware geometry as the single source of truth before `CanvasCompositor` (plan 19-03) creates its master canvas at FRAME_W×FRAME_H and before `_compositeAndPush` (plan 19-04) calls `buildHudTiles` (which validates `rgba.length === FRAME_W*FRAME_H*4`). A wrong geometry here cascades into buffer-length failures downstream (19-RESEARCH Pitfall 3).

Output: Updated constants + JSDoc + validation length in `hud-raster-frame.ts`; updated geometry assertions in `hud-raster-frame.test.ts` and `hud-poc-page.test.ts`.
</objective>
