<objective>
Land the single-command INV-1..5 verification suite that closes Phase 10 ROADMAP SC-5
(software half): `pnpm --filter @evf/validation-harness inv:all` runs the 5-invariant
sweep and produces a green/red table. The suite re-uses already-existing per-INV gates
(matchAsciiFixture fixtures for INV-1, the typecheck/biome/dead-code gates for INV-4,
the Phase 6 gesture-determinism test for INV-5) — this plan adds the ORCHESTRATOR, not
new verification logic.

Purpose: 6 milestones of accumulated invariants now need a single switch the field-test
operator (and CI) can flip before declaring "MVP healthy". Without this, regressions to
INV-1 layout integrity or INV-3 doc coherence can land silently because no human
manually runs all 5 gates in sequence.

Output: `inv-suite.ts` reusable module, `scripts/inv-all.ts` CLI, `pnpm inv:all` script,
unit tests proving each INV check correctly classifies its target state. Per D-Area1
the INV-2 step is a STUB (canonical-source-reachability ping) — full INV-2 cross-check
discipline remains a manual pre-bump procedure per `CLAUDE.md §Pre-bump checklist`.
</objective>
